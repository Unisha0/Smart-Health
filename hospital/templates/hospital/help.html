Help & Support

Managing Ambulance Requests

Follow these steps to manage ambulance requests efficiently:

  1. Login to your hospital admin account and go to the Ambulance Requests section.
  2. View the list of pending and active requests.
  3. Accept or decline requests based on availability.
  4. Assign an ambulance and update its status.

Updating Hospital Information

Keep hospital details up to date with these steps:

  • Navigate to the Hospital Info section in the dashboard.
  • Update contact details, available departments, and service hours.
  • Click Save to apply changes.

Managing Doctor Schedules

To update and manage doctors' schedules:

  • Go to the Doctor Management section.
  • Update availability, shifts, and appointment slots.
  • Save changes to update the live schedule.
